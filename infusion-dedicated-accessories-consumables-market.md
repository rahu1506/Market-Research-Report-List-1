Ambulatory infusion pumps are portable devices allowing patients to receive medication or fluids while on the move. PCA pumps are used for patient-controlled analgesia, enabling patients to control their pain medication dosage. Implantable infusion pumps are surgically implanted devices used to deliver medication directly into the body.</p></p>
