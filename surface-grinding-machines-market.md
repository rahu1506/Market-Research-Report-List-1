<p><p>Surface grinding machines are used in the metalworking industry to create smooth surfaces on workpieces. Horizontal-spindle machines have a horizontally oriented grinding wheel that moves along the length of the workpiece. Vertical-spindle machines have a vertically oriented grinding wheel that moves up and down. Single disc grinders use a single, rotating grinding wheel, while double-disc grinders have two opposing grinding wheels. These machines are used for precision grinding tasks, such as finishing flat surfaces or removing excess material.</p></p>
