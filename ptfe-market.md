<p><p>The PTFE market types refer to the different forms in which polytetrafluoroethylene is available for different industrial applications. Granular PTFE is in solid particle form and used for compression molding. Fine powder PTFE is finely ground and suitable for making lubricants, coatings, and insulation tapes. PTFE dispersion is a liquid form used for making coatings and impregnated fabrics. Micronized PTFE refers to ultra-fine particles used as additives in various industries such as plastics, paints, and inks. Each market type serves specific purposes in different industries.</p></p>
