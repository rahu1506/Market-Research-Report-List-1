<p><p>Coated fabrics refer to materials that have a layer of coating applied to their surface for enhanced durability, functionality, and aesthetics. Polymer coated fabrics are those where a synthetic polymer coating, like PVC or PU, is applied to the fabric. These coatings offer benefits such as water resistance, weatherproofing, and color retention. Rubber coated fabrics, on the other hand, have a layer of rubber applied to the fabric, offering properties like flexibility, abrasion resistance, and chemical resistance. "Others" in the market refer to different coatings applied to fabrics, including acrylic, silicone, or fluoropolymer coatings, each providing unique characteristics.</p></p>
