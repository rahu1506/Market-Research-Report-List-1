<p><p>Reactive PUR Hot-melt Adhesives consist of two types, Type I and Type II. Type I refers to adhesives that cure solely through heat, without the need for additional chemical activators. These adhesives are widely used in the automobile industry for bonding automotive components. On the other hand, Type II adhesives cure through both heat and an additional chemical activator. They are commonly utilized in the construction sector for applications such as wall panels and flooring, where a stronger and more durable bond is required.</p></p>
