<p><p>E-beam evaporation systems are commonly used in various industries for thin film deposition. The market for these systems is divided into three types: Single Pocket, Rotary Pocket, and Linear Pocket. </p><p>Single Pocket systems consist of a single crucible or pocket that holds the material to be evaporated. These are suitable for small-scale production and research applications.</p><p>Rotary Pocket systems feature multiple crucibles that rotate, allowing for simultaneous evaporation of different materials. This type is ideal for producing multilayered films or deposition of various materials.</p><p>Linear Pocket systems have an array of crucibles aligned in a linear fashion. They offer high throughput and are suitable for large-scale production, making them popular in industries such as semiconductors and optics.</p></p>
