<p><p>Metalworking fluid additives are substances added to metalworking fluids to enhance their performance and efficiency. The market for these additives is categorized into different types. Lubricity agents reduce friction between tools and workpieces, improving machining operations. Emulsifiers help in maintaining a stable mixture of oil and water in the fluid. Corrosion inhibitors prevent the metal surfaces from corroding. Defoamers eliminate foam formation during operations. Specialty amines, including biocides, have antimicrobial properties to prevent bacterial growth. And finally, there are other miscellaneous additives that serve various functions in metalworking processes.</p></p>
