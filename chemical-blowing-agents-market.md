<p><p>Chemical blowing agents are widely used in various industries to create a foaming effect in polymers, rubber, and plastics. The market for these agents can be categorized into three types - Azodicarbonamide (ADC), Hydrofluorocarbons (HFC), and Others. ADC blowing agents are commonly used due to their low cost and excellent foaming capabilities. HFC blowing agents, on the other hand, are preferred for their low ozone depletion potential. The "Others" category comprises a range of blowing agents, such as N,N-dinitrosopentamethylene tetramine (DNPT) and p-toluene sulfonyl hydrazide (TSH), which cater to specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
