<p><p>SBR latex is a type of synthetic rubber that is commonly used in various industries. There are two main types of SBR latex markets: Emulsion SBR latex and Solution SBR latex. Emulsion SBR latex is produced by emulsifying the rubber in water, resulting in a milky liquid that is used in applications such as paints, coatings, adhesives, and paper coatings. On the other hand, Solution SBR latex is produced by dissolving the rubber in a solvent, typically organic solvents, and is commonly used in applications such as rubber products, textiles, and carpet backing.</p></p>
