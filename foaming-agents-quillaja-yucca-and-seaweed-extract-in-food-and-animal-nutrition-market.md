In animal nutrition, yucca extract is added to feed to decrease ammonia emissions, improve digestion, and reduce intestinal microflora.</p><p>Seaweed extract, obtained from various types of seaweed, contains natural foaming agents, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
